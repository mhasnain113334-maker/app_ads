Delete Account Request - InRa App

If you want to delete your InRa account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the InRa app
  2. Go to your profile or settings
  3. Take a screenshot of your account page
  4. Send the screenshot with your request to our email

Send Request To

Email: mhasnain113334@gmail.com

Required Information

  • Your registered Google account email
  • Screenshot of your app profile or account
  • Request message: "Please delete my account"

What Will Be Deleted

  • User profile data
  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Firestore user data
  • App usage history

Data Retention

Some data may be kept for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes before permanent deletion.

Response Time

We usually process deletion requests within 3–7 business days.
